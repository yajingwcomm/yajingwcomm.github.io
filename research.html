Research Interests

I am fascinated by the intersection of emerging media technologies and socio-emotional functioning, approaching these topics through computational, experimental, survey-based, qualitative, and design-driven methods. My current research primarily focuses on human–AI interaction (HAII), with particular interest in its social and affective dimensions. I am especially intrigued to explore the human factors and psychological mechanisms that underlie HAII dynamics. Ultimately, my goal is to contribute to the development of evidence-based design interventions that better support effective, meaningful, and ethical human–AI partnerships.

My research interests include:

  • Human–Computer Interaction (HCI) & Human-Centered AI
  • AI-Mediated Communication (AI-MC)
  • Social & Affective Computing
  • Interaction & Experience Design / Research through Design (RtD)
  • Computational Social Science Methods

If you are interested in collaborating or discussing related topics, please feel free to contact me by email.
