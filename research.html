Research Interests

I am fascinated by the intersection of emerging media technologies and socio-emotional functioning, approaching these topics through computational, experimental, survey-based, qualitative methods, and design-oriented approaches. My current research primarily focuses on Human–AI Interaction (HAII), with particular interest in its social and affective dimensions. I am especially intrigued to dissect the human factors and psychological mechanisms that underlie HAII dynamics. Ultimately, my goal is to contribute to the development of evidence-based design interventions that promote effective, meaningful, and ethical human-AI interation, and suppor user agency and digital well-being.

My research interests include:

  • Human–Computer Interaction (HCI) & Human-Centered AI
  • AI-Mediated Communication (AI-MC)
  • Social & Affective Computing
  • Interaction & Experience Design / Research through Design (RtD)
  • Computational Social Science Methods
  • Digital trace data & Data donation

If you are interested in collaborating or discussing related topics, please feel free to contact me by email.
